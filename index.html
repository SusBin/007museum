Our exhibitions

Here you will find everything about the movie hero James Bond. You can see original movie posters, Cars, Motorcycle, Black Jack, Roulette, Gold Stacks, Gondola, Aircraft Cessna 172, Aston Martin, Jaguar E-Type, Izabella Scorupco Goldeneye Bikini, Omega Seamaster. We have a movable James Bond 007 Exhibition. Contact us here jamesbondexhibition@telia.com for more information about hosting a 007 Bond James exhibition in your country or city.

    Nybro is a center for some of the spy world’s Agent 007 James Bond’s most spectacular operations. The world’s only James Bond museum has a huge collection of memorabilia, perfect for any fan of 007.

    Opening hours

    Open mon-fri 11-15  sat. 10-14

    JAMES BOND MUSEUM is indoors about 1000 sqm with cinema etc. We have open WIFI that everyone can use in the museum

    PARKING: large parking for cars, motorhomes, caravans, buses, mopeds, motorcycle, bicycle is directly connected to the Bond 007 museum.

    PETS Animals are not allowed in the 007 museum, but there are green spaces outside and parking spaces in the shade.

    TOILETS, THE DISABILITY TOILET is in the museum.

    Prams Small strollers can be used in the museum and there are also areas for them outside and in the entrance. WHEELCHAIR, Permobil and Rollators l can be used without problems.
